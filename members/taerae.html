Kim Tae Rae (김태래) is a South Korean trainee under WAKEONE. He is a member of the boy group ZEROBASEONE. He is best known for being a contestant on the reality survival shows Top 10 Student and Boys Planet.

On the 12th episode of Boys Planet, Taerae was ranked 6th place- making him a member of ZEROBASEONE. Serving as ZB1's main vocalist, he never fails to shine brightly on stage and on camera!
