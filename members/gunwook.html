Park Gun Wook (박건욱) is a South Korean vocalist, rapper, and dancer under Jellyfish Entertainment. He is a member of the boy group ZEROBASEONE.

He is best known for his current participation in Mnet’s Boys Planet, however he initially gained popularity on another reality survival show 'Wild Idol'.

In the final episode of Boys Planet he was ranked no.5, making him a member of ZEROBASEONE (ZB1). He is known for his proficient skills in rapping, dancing, singing and matching different concepts. Hence, making him an all-rounder member!

Other than his natural talents, what makes Gunwook stand out is his history as a debate speech champion, vice/class president, surprising natural aegyo and impressive height (184cm!), he is known as a giant baby among others, a gap moe attractor for many fans and a giant sweet nerd with geonppandans (his fandom name).
